The Wharf, located in "scenic" D.C.!

Restaraunts and Bars

The Wharf hosts a large variety of different restaurants! Be on the lookout for new restraurants, like the Italian restaurant Lupo Marino, or the Blue Bottle Cafe! Kick back and relax with a drink at the Brighton Bar or the Cantina Bambina.

Events

Join us at the Wharf, for fun activities, such as Petalpalooza, a cherry blossom festival with things like a beer garden, roller rink, music, art, and s'more!

Shops

Shop along the waterfront, at well known stores like Ligne Roset, The Press, CVS, and other local joints like DC Rox and d/eleven. You can even shop for fine art at the Martha Spak Gallery.
